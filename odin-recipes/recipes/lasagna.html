Cheese Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • Noodles 2 pk
  • Olive Oil 2 tbsp
  • Sauce - 24 oz
  • Ricotta - 24 oz
  • Mozzerella - 2 Pounds

STEPS

  • Preheat oven to 450
  • Boil noodles - al dente
  • Layer pan with oil and sauce
  • Layer noodles
  • Layer cheeses
  • Repeat
  • Top layer - sauce and sprinkled cheese
  • Bake for 45 minutes
