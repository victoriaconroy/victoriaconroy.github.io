Cheese Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • Noodles 2 pk
  • Olive Oil 2 tbsp
  • Sauce - 24 oz
  • Ricotta - 24 oz
  • Mozzerella - 2 Pounds

STEPS

Preheat oven to 450

  1. Boil noodles - al dente
  2. Layer pan with oil and sauce
  3. Layer noodles
  4. Layer cheeses
  5. Repeat
  6. Top layer - sauce and sprinkled cheese
  7. Bake for 45 minutes
